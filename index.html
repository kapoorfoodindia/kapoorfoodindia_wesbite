Kapoor Food India

Kapoor Food India is a food processing company from Lucknow, manufacturing premium frozen foods under our brand Fasal. We source fresh raw materials from trusted farms and freeze it using IQF (Individual Quick Freezing) to lock in natural taste, texture, and nutrition.

Every batch goes through multiple quality checks, and we follow globally accepted export-ready practices. With strict cold storage and a reliable cold chain, Fasal products reach you safely and consistently—wherever you are.

Why Choose Kapoor Food India?

Farm Fresh

Sourced from trusted farms that follow our quality specifications—picked at peak freshness.

Quick Frozen

Blast reserve (for pulping) and IQF (for quick freezing)—preserve colour, taste, and texture, so every piece freezes individually and stays easy to use.

Quality Assured

Multiple in-process checks and hygienic handling to keep products clean, consistent, and true to their natural taste.

Reliable Delivery

Cold-chain storage and delivery partners keep your order protected from processing to doorstep.
