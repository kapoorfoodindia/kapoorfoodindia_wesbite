About Us

Discover Our Story and Commitment to Quality

Our Story

Kapoor Food India was founded with a vision to bring the finest frozen vegetables from the fertile lands around Lucknow to tables across India. Our journey began with a simple belief: everyone deserves access to fresh, nutritious vegetables year-round, regardless of the season.

Starting from humble beginnings in Lucknow, we have grown into a trusted name in the frozen food industry. Our commitment to quality, sustainable farming practices, and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success.

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality frozen vegetables that retain their farm-fresh taste and nutritional value, making healthy eating convenient and accessible for every household.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted frozen food brand, recognized for our commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainable practices that benefit both our customers and farming communities.

Our Core Values

Quality First

We never compromise on quality, ensuring every product meets our high standards

Trust & Transparency

Building lasting relationships through honest and transparent business practices

Sustainability

Supporting sustainable farming and minimizing our environmental footprint

Customer Focus

Listening to our customers and continuously improving to serve them better
