About Us

Discover Our Story and Commitment to Quality

Our Story

Kapoor Food India was founded in Lucknow with one clear vision: bring the finest farm produce to tables across India and global markets as safe, high-quality frozen foods under our brand Fasal. We started with a simple belief—everyone deserves access to nutritious, great-tasting products year-round, regardless of season.

From humble beginnings, we grew by focusing on what matters most: trusted sourcing, fast hygienic processing, and IQF (Individual Quick Freezing) that locks in natural taste, colour, and texture at peak freshness. We also preserve select raw materials carefully for rightful processing, keeping quality and taste uncompromised.

Today, our manufacturing setup is designed to reduce manual handling and improve consistency. Teams operate and monitor the line in controlled areas, while automated systems help maintain repeatable hygiene and quality standards. We are proud of a female-centric workforce and an opportunity-first culture.

Our Mission

To deliver safe, high-quality Fasal frozen foods that preserve natural taste and nutrition—making healthy cooking convenient for homes, and dependable for food businesses and distribution partners.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted Indian frozen-food manufacturer and partner—known for export-ready standards, transparent relationships, and responsible operations that support customers, distributors, and farming communities.

Our Process

From Farm to Freezer: Our Quality Journey

1

Sourcing

We source from trusted farms that follow our quality requirements—selected at peak harvest for best taste and texture.

2

Processing

Fast hygienic processing with multiple checks to remove foreign matter and ensure clean, safe raw material before freezing.

3

Freezing

IQF quickly freezes each piece individually. Products are maintained in a strict cold range (typically -25°C to -18°C, product-dependent).

4

Packaging

Food-grade packaging on controlled lines to reduce handling—sealed to protect freshness, hygiene, and shelf life.

5

Delivery

Cold-chain movement and delivery through reefer van to ensure products arrive safely without temperature abuse or tampering.
